I analyzed the structure of your uploaded **TopRankr backend**. Your project has evolved beyond a simple ATS—it's now an **AI-powered recruiter operating system**.

The best way to build the frontend is **not by pages**, but by **backend domain layers**. This keeps the frontend aligned with your existing services, APIs, Celery workflows, and database models.

# Your Current Backend Architecture

```text
Authentication Layer
        ↓
Recruiter Layer
        ↓
Search Pipeline
        ↓
AI Ranking Pipeline
        ↓
Candidate Layer
        ↓
Comparison Layer
        ↓
Learning Layer
        ↓
Analytics Layer
        ↓
Security Layer
        ↓
Infrastructure Layer
```
